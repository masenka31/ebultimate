# Archive

## 2025

2025 season is all done and here you can view the final results of tournaments.


{{< timeline2 >}}

{{< timeline-entry2 title="Vienna Spring Break" date="3. 5. 2025" >}}

The first ranking tournament didn't go as planned. We had a few ups and downs and only started to glue together as a completely new team. We had a lot of fun, but also suffered a heart-break: the loss to 3SB meant we did not qualify to the WUCC 2026. Our ranking suffered and we fell all the way down to 24th place at one point. The journey to improve started there.

{{< /timeline-entry2 >}}

{{< timeline-entry2 title="Windmill" date="25. 7. 2025" >}}

Windmill is fun and competitive and we had a lot to prove. The glue finally sticked and we managed to win game after game to get ourselves high in the swiss system. Afterall, we were able to secure a bronze medal! And we were hungry to prove we are a top team in Europe.

{{< /timeline-entry2 >}}

{{< timeline-entry2 title="Summer Tour Wroclaw" date="16. 8. 2025" >}}

Summer Tour was all about improving our ranking to get to EUCF. We managed to do so on the first day after winning all of our games on Saturday. Sunday brought us to the finals against Jinx where we showed what we're made of: pushing Jinx all the way to a universe point. We might have lost the game, but we gained a hell lot of confidence.

{{< /timeline-entry2 >}}

{{< timeline-entry2 title="EUCF" date="26. 9. 2025" >}}

EUCF, the highlight of the season. After Summer Tour, we were pumped to show what we're worth. And we did. We managed to keep up with GRUT in the pool play, won the pre-quarters and got to the quarters, which was the main goal for the tournament. Unfortunately, we faced BFD Shout and even though we managed to keep up in the first half, Shout was able to crush us in the second half and sent us to fight for the 5th place. Our spirits were a bit down and we could not keep up with our opponents anymore. We finished at the 8th place, but still an amazing achievement for a team that was doubted the whole season.

{{< /timeline-entry2 >}}
