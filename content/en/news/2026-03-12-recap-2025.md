After the hard grind of the 2024 EUCS, East Block went through a lot of changes. We switched coaches, lost the captain, Kristyna Tlusta to GRUT, another amazing player in Tereza Havelcova moved on to play for Jinx and a lot of other players decided to either not play or take a year off. In consequence, we lost 11 players that played in 2024 and we knew that the next season would be hard.

### Tryouts

Everything started with tryouts: an amazing success of 35 players joining us for the first meet of the season. We had fun and practices our motion offence with training plan set-up by our new coach Lu Burgess. Amazing new talent joined the team and we knew the future was bright.

![](/images/news/2025/tryouts/DSC07605.jpg)

### Vienna Spring Break

The first ranking tournament was to be an important challenge: how fast can we glue the team together? Unfortunately, not fast enough. We managed to win a couple games, but lost the crucial game against 3SB that decided who's gonna represent Czech women at the WUCC in 2026.

The heartbreak continued with other tough games and the ranking was devastating: at one point (ranking 2025-06-03) we fell all the way down to 24th place. The only way back up was to perform well at Summer Tour and show everyone that we deserve to still be counted among the top European teams.

![](/images/news/2025/2025-vsb.jpg)

### Windmill

We needed something to take the pressure of and what better way than to go to the best European tournament for preparation - Windmill? We didn't have high expectations, we just wanted to play the best ultimate possible. And we did.

3 wins and one close loss in universe point put us into the top 4 and into the semifinals. Unfortunately, we lost the game to Tokyo Maverics 9:15 and went to play for the 3rd place. It was a tough game, but we battled up until the end, taking it to the universe points and eventually winning 15:14 against Colombia's Aerosoul Ultimate Club.

We are sad there's no picture because it got lost somewhere in the SD card space... but we had a lot of fun and the win gave us enough confidence for the Summer Tour fast approaching.

![](/images/news/2025/2025-windmill.jpg)

### Summer Tour

Time to shine, time to bring out the big guns. We knew we had to win as many games as possible with the highest point difference as possible. We managed to get 3 important wins on Saturday and after just one day, we were able to jump in the rankings all the way up to 11th place and we were sure that we just qualified for EUCF, but more work was to be done.

On Sunday, we snatched two more wins and moved to the finals against Jinx who were so far cruising the tournament with obvious wins. After a little rought start, we managed to keep in the game, eventually bringing the game to another universe point. We didn't get the win, but our confidence jumped to all time high. We were back and ready for the final push at EUCF.

![](/images/news/2025/2025-summer-tour.JPG)

### EUCF

We knew that EUCF would be tough. The ranking system and pool creation was harsh on a few teams, including us, but we wanted to fight. We finished 2nd in our pool, managing a win against Spice and 3SB, and a loss with one good half against GRUT. We faced Bristol in the pre-quarterfinals and with a confident win we moved to be a top 8 team again!

Unfortunately, we couldn't stop BFD Shout in the quarterfinals and lost too much of our confidence and strenght for the next matches. We finished 8th overall, higher than most people expected. We are proud of the achievements we made and the team we have build in just one short season.
